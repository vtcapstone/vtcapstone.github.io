Seymour Mountain (First peak)

Completed on 26/11/2023 and 16/01/2024

7 km round trip challenging hike with 460 meters of elevation.

Beautiful way to explore Mount Seymour with very nice views at Brockton Point and First Peak. Bring microspikes in winter as it is steep with a lot of snow. Start at the end of Mount Seymour Road.

No public transit option.
