Belcarra Bluffs

Completed on March 24 2024

X

6km round trip, intermediate hike

Fun hike near the city with an impressive view.

Follow Springboard Trail until you reach a wooden park sign. 10-15 metres after, turn right on an unmarked trail that brings you all the way until the viewpoint. Return with the Admiralty Point Trail following the water to complete the loop.

Public transit option: take the Millenium Line to Moody Centre, then take the 182 to WB Bedweel Bay Rd @ Midden Rd. The bus brings you to the beach parking, where the start of the hike is.
