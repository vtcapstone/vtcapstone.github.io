Norvan Falls

Completed on 03/05/2024

X

14 km intermediate hike, around 200 meters of elevation.

Nice hike in North Vancouver ending at the powerful Norvan Falls. The trail is well maintained and easy to follow.

Public Transit Option: Take the #210 bus (Upper Lynn Valley) until EB McNair Dr @ Ramsay Rd. Walk about 15 minutes to the Lynn Headwaters Entrance Parking Lot.
