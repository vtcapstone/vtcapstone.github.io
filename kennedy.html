Kennedy Falls and Big Cedar Trail

Completed on 28/03/2024

X

10 km round trip, 150 meters of elevation, intermediate hike.

This hike brings you to the Big Cedar Tree (~600-years old) about halfway and ends at Kennedy Falls. Be sure to follow the orange markers in order not to lose the trail. It might be more challenging to cross the creeks in spring due to more water.

Public Transit Option: Either take the #210 bus from downtown Vancouver to the top of Mountain Highway or take the Seabus to North Vancouver, then the #228 bus to Lynn Valley Centre Shopping Mall and finally the #210 bus to Coleman Street.
