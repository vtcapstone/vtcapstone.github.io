About Ève's Capstone Project

2023-2024

This project captures the remarkable hiking experiences I had during my year living in Vancouver (September 2023 - July 2024). While compiling this collection of trails, I reflected on unforgettable adventures with friends and family. Building this website improved my skills in HTML and CSS while linking my academic interest in computer science with my love for hiking. As I prepare for graduation and returning to Montreal, I’m grateful to have this record of the incredible landscapes I encountered in BC. I hope others enjoy reading about my adventures and use this guide for their next visit in this beautiful region.
