Halfmoon Beach Hike in Golden Ears Park

Completed on 21/04/2024

X

20 km round trip, intermediate hike with barely any elevation.

Option to camp at Halfmoon Beach to make it a backpacking experience. Use the East Canyon Trail that makes you pass by Lower Falls and View Point Beach. Return by the West Canyon Trail back to the parking lot.

No public transit option.
