Sea to Summit Hike

Completed on 29/03/2024

X

10 km challenging hike with 1100 m of elevation.

Very demanding hike in Squamish bringing you to the top of the Sea to Sky Gondola. This trail is well marked and offers beautiful views. Take the Gondola to go back down as the trail is too steep to use it on the way back.

No public transit option, use the service shuttle Squamish Connector that drops you off at the base of the Sea to Sky Gondola.
