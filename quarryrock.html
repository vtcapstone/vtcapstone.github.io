Quarry Rock Hike

Completed on 29/10/2023

X

4 km round trip easy hike with 100 meters of elevation.

Easy hike close to the city, in Deep Cove, with a nice viewpoint. The trail is well maintained and easy to follow.

Public Transit Option: Take the #211 bus (Seymour) to SB Panorama Dr @ Naughton Ave and walk 5 minutes to the trail start.
