Garibaldi Lake

Completed on 31/12/2023

X

18.7 km round trip, 850 meters of elevation, intermediate hike.

Incredible hike in Garibaldi Provincial Park with a stunning view at the glacier lake. Bring microspikes if part of the trail still has snow. Start at the Rubble Creek Parking Lot.

No public transit option, option to use Parkbus as a shuttle service.
