Lynn Valley

Completed on 02/09/2023 and 26/03/2024

X

Multiple easy trails in this park of North Vancouver.

Must see park near Vancouver with several attractions including Twin Falls, the 30-foot pool and a suspension bridge. The famous Baden-Powell Trail goes through Lynn Valley allowing you to go explore other parts of North Vancouver.

Public Transit Option: Take the #211/210 bus and switch to the #227 at NB Mountain Hwy @ Fern St. Get off at WB Peters Rd @ Duval Rd and walk 5 minutes to the Lynn Canyon Ecology Centre.
