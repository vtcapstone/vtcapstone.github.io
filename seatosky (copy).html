Stawamus Chief First Peak

Completed on 30/03/2024

X

4 km round trip very challenging hike with 550 meters of elevation.

Getting to the first peak of the Stawamus Chief is challenging but offers a very impressive view. Be prepared to climb with chains and ladders and do not go on a rainy day.

No public transit option,use the service shuttle Squamish Connector that drops you off at the base of the Sea to Sky Gondola.
